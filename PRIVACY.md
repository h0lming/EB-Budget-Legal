# Privacy Policy — EB Budget

_Last updated: 11 June 2026_

EB Budget ("the App") is developed and published by El-bjørn (CVR 44817403), Denmark. This policy explains how the App handles your data.

## Short version

The App is free and stores everything you enter **locally on your device**. El-bjørn does **not** collect, receive, sell or share your personal or financial data.

## 1. Data you enter

Salary figures, benefits, deductions, expenses and budget years that you enter are stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage. This data never leaves your device through the App and is not accessible to El-bjørn. If you delete the App, this data is removed with it.

## 2. Data we do not collect

El-bjørn operates no server for the App and collects no analytics, usage data, advertising identifiers, contact information or location data. The App contains no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

## 3. Children

The App is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.

## 4. Your rights

Because El-bjørn does not hold your personal data, there is no server-side data for us to access, export or delete — you control all data on your device. Under the GDPR you retain your rights as a data subject. For any question, contact us below. You may also lodge a complaint with the Danish Data Protection Agency (Datatilsynet, Carl Jacobsens Vej 35, 2500 Valby, datatilsynet.dk).

## 5. Changes

We may update this policy. The current version is always available at the link in the App.
