# Privacy Policy — EB Budget

_Last updated: 9 June 2026_

EB Budget ("the App") is developed and published by El-bjørn (CVR 44817403), Denmark. This policy explains how the App handles your data.

## Short version

The App stores everything you enter **locally on your device**. El-bjørn does **not** collect, receive, sell or share your personal or financial data.

## 1. Data you enter

Salary figures, benefits, deductions, expenses and budget years that you enter are stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage (SwiftData). This data never leaves your device through the App and is not accessible to El-bjørn.

If you delete the App, this locally stored data is removed with it.

## 2. Data we do not collect

El-bjørn does not operate a server for the App and does not collect analytics, usage data, advertising identifiers, contact information or location data.

## 3. Subscriptions and Apple

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. El-bjørn does not receive your payment details. Apple may process purchase and account information in accordance with Apple's own Privacy Policy. To manage your subscription, use your Apple Account settings.

## 4. Third parties

The App does not include third-party advertising or third-party analytics SDKs.

## 5. Children

The App is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.

## 6. Your rights

Because El-bjørn does not hold your personal data, there is no server-side data for us to access, export or delete. You control all data directly within the App and on your device. Under the GDPR you retain your rights as a data subject; for any question, contact us below.

## 7. Changes

We may update this policy. The current version is always available at the link in the App.
